The Best Movies According to Mia

My Top 3 Movies of All Time

Perfect Blue

Excellent psychological thriller where you see the main character slowly be driven to insanity

Howl's Moving Castle

Beautifully magical Studio Ghibli movie that covers the themes of loving others and yourself for who you are regardless of appearance.

I Want to Eat Your Pancreas

A heartwrenging but beautiful story of a girl with pancreas cancer living her life to the fullest and staying positive even in the face of an eminent death
